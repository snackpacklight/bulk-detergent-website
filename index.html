Our Story

Built for Real Families,
Not Big Retail

CC Tubs & Suds started with one simple idea: bulk liquid laundry detergent shouldn't cost a fortune. We're a local, family-run business that knows how fast household costs add up — and we wanted to do something about it.

By selling direct to our customers in large 5-gallon buckets, we cut out the middleman and pass the savings straight to you. Our liquid detergent is comparable to leading national brands in cleaning performance — without the name-brand markup.

Whether you're a busy family, running a small business, or just tired of buying small bottles every week — we've got a better way. One bucket. Real savings. Clean clothes.
